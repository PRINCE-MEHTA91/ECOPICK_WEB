Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is EcoPick?

EcoPick is a platform that uses AI to identify recyclable waste and estimate its value, making recycling more rewarding.

How does it work?

You can upload or take a picture of your waste material, and our AI will analyze it, identify the type, and provide an estimated price based on current market rates.

Is it free to use?

Yes, EcoPick is free for individual users.

Who can use EcoPick?

EcoPick is available for users aged 18 years or above. By using the platform, you confirm that the information you provide is accurate and genuine.

Accuracy of AI Estimates

The price and material identification provided by EcoPick are AI-based estimates. Actual prices may vary depending on local market conditions and waste quality.

User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for ensuring that uploaded images are clear and represent real recyclable waste. Misuse, fake uploads, or fraudulent activities are strictly prohibited.

Prohibited Activities

Users must not upload harmful, illegal, misleading, or non-recyclable content. Any attempt to manipulate results or misuse the platform may result in account suspension.

Third-Party Services

EcoPick may connect users with third-party recyclers or collectors. EcoPick is not responsible for disputes, delays, or transactions between users and third parties.

Data Privacy

We respect your privacy. Uploaded images and user data are handled securely and used only to improve our services. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.

Account Termination

EcoPick reserves the right to suspend or terminate user accounts that violate our terms, policies, or engage in misuse of the platform.

Changes to These Terms

EcoPick may update these terms from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes means you accept the updated terms.

Contact & Support

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us at support@ecopick.com.
